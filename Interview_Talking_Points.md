# Executive Project Overview

## Project Title

Retail Enterprise Performance Analytics & Customer Intelligence Dashboard

## Business Case

A national retail enterprise needs a unified BI platform that connects store sales, e-commerce demand, returns, inventory, product margin, and customer behavior. Current reporting is fragmented across POS exports, CRM files, merchandising reports, and inventory snapshots. Leadership needs one trusted analytical model for weekly trading reviews, customer growth planning, and operations decision-making.

## Problem Statement

The business cannot consistently answer high-value questions such as:

- Which regions are increasing revenue but losing margin?
- Which customer segments are repeating, churning, or under-engaged?
- Which products drive profitable growth versus high return cost?
- Which stores have stockout risk, overstock risk, or fulfillment issues?
- Which strategic opportunities should leadership prioritize next quarter?

## Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Decision Need |
|---|---|
| CEO / Executive Team | Revenue growth, profitability, risk, and strategic opportunities. |
| CFO / Finance | Margin, cost, discount leakage, sales target achievement, and forecast outlook. |
| COO / Operations | Fulfillment, stock status, regional execution, and inventory risk. |
| CMO / CRM Team | Customer segmentation, retention, churn, and customer lifetime value. |
| Merchandising | Category performance, top/bottom products, return causes, and markdown needs. |
| Regional Directors | Store and territory performance against enterprise benchmarks. |

## Expected Business Outcomes

- Create a single source of truth for retail performance.
- Reduce manual reporting effort through reusable Power BI semantic modeling.
- Improve retention campaigns using repeat purchase and churn indicators.
- Improve gross margin by identifying discount, cost, and return leakage.
- Reduce stockout and overstock exposure with inventory health scoring.
- Enable leadership to act on regional opportunities with drill-through reporting.

## KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Sample Value |
|---|---|---:|
| Total Revenue | Sum of net sales after discounts. | $23,522.38 |
| Total Profit | Revenue minus cost of goods sold. | $9,224.05 |
| Profit Margin % | Profit divided by revenue. | 39.2% |
| Total Orders | Distinct order IDs. | 50 |
| Average Order Value | Revenue divided by orders. | $470.45 |
| Customer Count | Distinct purchasing customers. | 32 |
| Repeat Customer % | Customers with more than one order divided by customers. | 56.2% |
| Return Rate % | Return amount divided by sales amount. | 15.7% |
| Inventory Risk Count | Inventory rows marked Critical or Reorder. | 14 |
| Forecast Revenue | Expected revenue based on recent trend/rolling logic. | Implemented in DAX |
